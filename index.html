About me

I completed a PhD in biological modelling at UCL in 2012. After graduating, I worked as a researcher and project manager at Oxford University’s Blavatnik School of Government, where I was part of the small administrative team that set up the School and its academic programme. For the last four years I worked in Product for a fast-growing tech startup – Arachnys – where I led a small, expert research team in developing and maintaining a best-in-class library of intelligence sources in 96 languages.

I have a solution-oriented and creative approach to problem solving and am always looking for ways to make things work better, through empowering team members and improving processes. In addition to effective and diplomatic project management, I enjoy creating and delivering training materials and coaching. I generally work most effectively bridging between technical teams and non-tech stakeholders.
